ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Kamilla, I'm 25 years old and come from Odense. I am a "livsnyder" a Danish saying meaning someone who appreciates the little things in life. The taste of a good cup of coffee, the smell of freshly baked buns or the breeze coming from an open window.

I have always been creative and had a flair for digital design. My main interests are in UI/UX design and writing. I have skills in various Adobe programs; lightroom, XD, indesign, photoshop, illustrator and premiere pro. I also know CSS and HTML on a base level.

Fun fact:

At my wedding in 2021, I designed everything from save-the-date, and invitations to name tags and menu cards.

Also:

From 2015-2018 I ran a travel blog while traveling in Southeast Asia, USA and also some parts of Europe.
